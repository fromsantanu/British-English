# Restaurant

Here is a list of 20 words in the context of "Restaurant," along with their meanings and usage examples:

1. **Menu**  
   *Meaning*: A list of food and drink options offered in a restaurant.  
   *Usage*: "The menu had a wide range of dishes, from pasta to seafood."

2. **Waiter/Waitress**  
   *Meaning*: A person who serves food and drinks to customers in a restaurant.  
   *Usage*: "The waiter recommended the house special when we couldn't decide what to order."

3. **Reservation**  
   *Meaning*: A booking made in advance to secure a table at a restaurant.  
   *Usage*: "We made a reservation at the restaurant to ensure we had a table for dinner."

4. **Appetizer**  
   *Meaning*: A small dish served before the main course to stimulate the appetite.  
   *Usage*: "We started our meal with a delicious shrimp cocktail as an appetizer."

5. **Entrée**  
   *Meaning*: The main course of a meal in a restaurant.  
   *Usage*: "For my entrée, I ordered grilled salmon with vegetables."

6. **Dessert**  
   *Meaning*: A sweet course served at the end of a meal.  
   *Usage*: "The chocolate mousse dessert was the perfect way to end the meal."

7. **Cuisine**  
   *Meaning*: A style or method of cooking, especially as characteristic of a particular country or region.  
   *Usage*: "The restaurant is known for its authentic Italian cuisine."

8. **Chef**  
   *Meaning*: A professional cook who manages the kitchen and prepares meals.  
   *Usage*: "The chef prepared a special dish for us that wasn’t on the menu."

9. **Ambience**  
   *Meaning*: The atmosphere or mood of a restaurant, often created by décor, lighting, and music.  
   *Usage*: "The dim lighting and soft music created a romantic ambience in the restaurant."

10. **Buffet**  
    *Meaning*: A style of serving food where guests serve themselves from a variety of dishes.  
    *Usage*: "We enjoyed the Sunday brunch buffet, which featured a wide selection of breakfast and lunch items."

11. **Specialty**  
    *Meaning*: A particular dish or type of food that a restaurant is known for.  
    *Usage*: "The restaurant’s specialty is wood-fired pizza, which attracts a lot of customers."

12. **Takeout**  
    *Meaning*: Food purchased from a restaurant to be eaten elsewhere.  
    *Usage*: "We didn’t feel like dining in, so we ordered takeout from our favorite restaurant."

13. **Bill**  
    *Meaning*: The total amount of money owed for the meal, presented to the customer at the end of the dining experience.  
    *Usage*: "The waiter brought the bill after we finished our dessert."

14. **Tip**  
    *Meaning*: An extra amount of money given to a waiter or waitress for their service.  
    *Usage*: "We left a generous tip because the service was excellent."

15. **Tablecloth**  
    *Meaning*: A cloth used to cover a table, often used in restaurants to create a formal setting.  
    *Usage*: "The restaurant had white tablecloths and elegant place settings."

16. **Host/Hostess**  
    *Meaning*: The person who greets customers at the entrance of a restaurant and shows them to their table.  
    *Usage*: "The hostess greeted us with a smile and seated us near the window."

17. **Wine list**  
    *Meaning*: A menu or selection of wines offered at a restaurant.  
    *Usage*: "We asked to see the wine list to choose a bottle that would pair well with our meal."

18. **Cuisine**  
    *Meaning*: The particular style of cooking associated with a region or country.  
    *Usage*: "The restaurant specializes in French cuisine, offering dishes like coq au vin and crème brûlée."

19. **Sous chef**  
    *Meaning*: The second-in-command in a restaurant kitchen, responsible for assisting the head chef.  
    *Usage*: "The sous chef prepared many of the sauces and side dishes that complemented the main courses."

20. **Patio dining**  
    *Meaning*: Eating outside on a terrace or patio, often available in restaurants during pleasant weather.  
    *Usage*: "We opted for patio dining to enjoy the beautiful summer evening while we ate."

These words cover various elements of the dining experience, including the staff, the food, and the atmosphere of a restaurant.
